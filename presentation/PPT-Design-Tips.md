# PPT Design Tips

## Overall Style

- Use soft pastel colors instead of dark corporate colors
- Use rounded rectangles and pill-shaped labels
- Keep plenty of white space
- Add tiny phone, app, cart, and checkmark icons

## Suggested Color Palette

- Mint: `#BEEAD7`
- Sky Blue: `#BFDFFF`
- Soft Yellow: `#FFF1B8`
- Peach: `#FFD6C9`
- Lavender Gray: `#E9E4F5`

## Suggested Fonts

- Title: `Poppins` or `Nunito`
- Body: `Aptos`, `Calibri`, or `Open Sans`

## Cute But Professional

- Avoid too many stickers or decorations
- Use one small illustration per slide
- Keep text concise and airy
- Let the diagrams do part of the talking

## Best Visuals To Add

- User journey arrows
- Simple architecture diagram
- Entity relationship mini-graph
- API endpoint cards
- A tiny “owned” badge on purchased apps
